The Kitchen Chronicles

"Cooking has always been my first language. Long before I learned to write code, I learned to read flavors — to understand how ingredients talk to each other, how heat transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary."

My passion for food isn't just about following recipes. It's about experimentation, creativity, and the joy of sharing. Every dish I make carries a little piece of my story — my culture, my curiosity, and my relentless desire to create something beautiful.

And interestingly, the kitchen taught me the same lessons that coding would later reinforce: precision matters, creativity matters more, and the best results come from fearless iteration.
